Partners


Partners are an important factor to our success. They help us bring new ideas, new projects and new perspective to our activities. They also help us create awareness of our mission and help further our goal of providing a good quality of life for every human being. We are currently enaged with the following partners:
Asha For Education, a non-profit organization based in Florida, USA, that works on promoting basic education in India. Asha partially supports some of our educational endeavours. See documents
The Paul K Feyerabend Foundation is a french non-profit that is funding a part of our Sacred Groves project for rejuvenating community forests in Thenur. See documents

We are always looking for partners from other Non Government Organizations (NGOs), Non Profit Organizations (NPOs), and companies in the public and private sector to help us with our projects. Please send an email to info@payir.org if you want to partner with Payir or need additional information.
